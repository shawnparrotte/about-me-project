About Me

What can I say about myself. Do I even know what I'm all about? And at what level do I try to even describe the person that is me. Right now I'm on a mission of sorts. To see how much an average dude can learn in a year. How much of an affect he can have on the world, if he desired to add some signal to the noise of what's around us. I feel like some kind of idealist. Can you say that I'm an idealist that works in the fabric of the real? Actually coming in contact with the reality of the world we live in and making an attempt to gain a broad perspective on what is going on and learning how to steer things a bit.

It sure is an egoic gesture to "steer" things. As if I know any better. But why not attempt to know? To do the work that is necessary to actually see things for what they are. God. There I go again. Assuming that I know that there is a veil of some kind over things. What is it? Ideology. It will always be there. Maybe one of my missions will be to gain a deeper understanding of the ideologies that I come in contact with and that are the invisible hands that steer us? I don't know. but this was a good point to start the work.
